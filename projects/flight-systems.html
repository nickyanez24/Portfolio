Flight Systems Hardware

Manufacturing, integration, and assembly of high-powered rocket systems.

Overview

As a member of Longhorn Rocketry's Flight Systems team, I contributed to the fabrication, assembly, integration, and preparation of high-powered rockets for testing and competition. The role focused on transforming engineering designs into flight-ready hardware through manufacturing and subsystem integration.

What I Worked On

  • Participated in composite fabrication and layup operations.
  • Performed sanding, cutting, drilling, fitting, and bonding operations.
  • Assisted with airframe assembly and subsystem integration.
  • Supported recovery system installation and verification.
  • Conducted hardware fit checks and alignment inspections.
  • Used OpenRocket to evaluate vehicle stability and flight characteristics.
  • Supported launch preparation and competition operations.

Technical Highlights

  • Worked directly with flight hardware throughout the manufacturing process.
  • Developed experience with composite structures and rocket assembly.
  • Learned the importance of tolerances, alignment, and subsystem integration.
  • Contributed to rockets used in testing and competition environments.

Tools Used

Composite fabrication, OpenRocket, mechanical assembly, recovery systems, flight hardware integration, manufacturing processes
