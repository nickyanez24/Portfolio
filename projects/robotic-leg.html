Robotic Leg Design, Control, and Kinematics

Mechanical design, actuator integration, kinematics, and robotic control.

Overview

This project focused on the design, manufacturing, assembly, modeling, and control of a three-degree-of-freedom robotic leg. The project combined mechanical design, actuator integration, kinematic modeling, simulation, and control system development.

What I Worked On

  • Performed structural and load analysis of robotic leg components.
  • Manufactured and assembled components using 3D printing and laser cutting.
  • Integrated and configured Dynamixel servo actuators.
  • Experimentally tuned PD controller gains.
  • Implemented forward kinematics models in Python.
  • Validated kinematic solutions using simulation and hardware.
  • Worked with PyBullet to visualize robotic motion.

Technical Highlights

  • Connected physical hardware development with mathematical robot modeling.
  • Applied kinematics concepts to predict end-effector position.
  • Investigated actuator performance through control gain tuning.
  • Compared simulation results against physical robot behavior.

Tools Used

Python, PyBullet, robotics, forward kinematics, servo integration, PD control, 3D printing, laser cutting
